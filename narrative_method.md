# Narrative Method (行文叙事方法)

## Voice & Tone

- **Authoritative yet approachable**: Expert guidance delivered as peer advice
- **Personal and authentic**: First-person narratives from real entrepreneurial experience
- **Action-oriented**: Every concept tied to concrete, executable steps
- **Empathetic**: Acknowledges reader fears and doubts (especially about leaving jobs, selling, public visibility)
- **Chinese internet context**: References 知乎, 公众号, 小红书, 抖音, 知识星球, 生财有术, etc.

## Opening Patterns

### Pattern 1: Personal Origin Story
Begin with author's personal journey:
> 我从大二开始经营自媒体，毕业后直接成为一家一人公司，独立跑通商业闭环...

### Pattern 2: Market Observation
Start with an observable trend:
> 这个世界上有种神奇的组织，它存在的目标就是追求利润、创造财富...

### Pattern 3: Problem-Agitation
Highlight a pain point readers relate to:
> 现在聊起公司，很多人的认知还停留在"做大、做强"的阶段...

### Pattern 4: Contrarian Insight
Challenge conventional wisdom:
> 一人公司不是自由职业。自由职业是出售自己的技能，直接获取收益...

## Argumentation Structure

### Layer 1: Concept Introduction
Define the concept in accessible Chinese business terms. Use analogies from daily life or well-known companies.

### Layer 2: Personal Case Study
Share author's own experience as primary evidence:
- Specific numbers (25岁赚到第一个一百万)
- Specific platforms (知乎引流5万个粉丝)
- Specific timelines (2016年开始, 7年经验)

### Layer 3: Reader Application
Translate the lesson into actionable steps for the reader:
- "你也可以..."
- "具体怎么做呢？"
- "以下是我的建议..."

### Layer 4: Broader Framework
Connect the specific example to a general principle or model:
- Reference framework diagrams (五力模型, 商业闭环)
- Use formulas (自媒体收入=产品价格×(私域数量×购买率)×推出产品的数量)

## Storytelling Techniques

### The "Iceberg" Metaphor
> 我们最容易看到的商业的冰山一角就是产品，但隐藏在冰山之下的还有很多东西...

### The "1 and 0" Analogy
> 如果把专业能力比作"1"，那么后面的流量、销售、供应链、品牌等能力其实就是"1"后面的"0"...

### Platform-Specific Examples
Always ground advice in specific Chinese platforms:
- 知乎 for long-form content and traffic
- 公众号 for private domain and monetization
- 小红书 for visual content and female audience
- 抖音 for short video and mass reach
- 知识星球 for community and paid content
- 微信群 for private traffic and sales

### Anonymized Third-Party Cases
Reference other entrepreneurs without naming them:
> 比如我的公司合伙人阿猫，他毕业后就没去公司上班...
> 曾经有人在知乎上问过一个问题...

## Section Conclusion Patterns

### Key Takeaway Summary
End with a short summary of the core lesson.

### Transition Hook
Bridge to the next section:
> 掌握一人公司的五力模型——流量能力、产品能力、营销能力、生态能力、IP能力，你也能够做到。

### Action Prompt
Leave reader with a specific next step.

## Language Characteristics

### Chinese Business Vocabulary
Use authentic Chinese entrepreneurship terminology:
- 商业闭环 (business closed loop)
- 跑通 (run through successfully)
- 变现 (monetize)
- 私域/公域 (private/public domain traffic)
- 裂变 (viral growth/fission)
- 群发售 (group launch)
- 互推 (mutual promotion)
- 超级个体 (super individual)

### Rhetorical Questions
Frequently used to engage reader:
> 既然如此，我们为什么非要去公司上班呢？
> 你创办公司的目的是什么？

### Colloquial Expressions
Mix formal business language with casual Chinese:
- "搞流量" (get traffic)
- "螺丝钉" (cog in the machine)
- "手里拿锤子，看什么都像钉子"
- "老板让我做什么，我就做什么"

### Numerical Examples
Use concrete calculations to prove points:
> 假设我每天加10个微信好友，一年下来就有3600多个好友...
> 只要每年我能卖出36份，就可以赚3600元...
