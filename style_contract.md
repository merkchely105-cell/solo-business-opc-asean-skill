## Typography System

### Font Families
- **Reference fonts detected**: FZFSK (方正仿宋), FZKTK (方正楷体), SimSun (宋体)
- **Body text**: SimSun (宋体) or Noto Serif CJK SC -- 10.5pt-12pt
- **Headings**: Noto Sans CJK SC Bold or SimHei (黑体) -- 14pt-22pt
- **Chapter titles**: Large sans-serif CJK -- 22-28pt, centered
- **Section titles**: Medium sans-serif CJK -- 16-18pt, centered
- **Captions**: Serif CJK -- 9-10pt, centered below figures

### Typography Rules
- All Chinese text uses full-width punctuation
- Chapter labels "第X章" centered at top, slightly smaller than chapter title
- Section labels "第X节" centered above section title
- Body text: 1.5-1.8 line height for Chinese text
- Paragraph spacing: 0.5-1em between paragraphs
- No indentation at paragraph start (Chinese book style with spacing)

## Page Layout

### Book Style (Reference 1)
- A4 page size (210mm x 297mm)
- Margins: top 25mm, bottom 25mm, left 25mm, right 25mm
- Page numbers: bottom-right corner, starting after front matter
- Chapter opener pages: no page number, title centered vertically in upper half
- Generous whitespace around headings and diagrams

### Guide Style (Reference 2)
- A4 page size
- Wider margins for clean modern look
- Photo/illustration at top of cover page
- Emoji markers for section highlights
- Yellow background callout boxes with border-radius
- Checkbox items with unicode checkbox characters
